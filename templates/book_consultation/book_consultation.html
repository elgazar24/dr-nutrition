Frequently Asked Questions

During your first consultation, our nutrition specialist will conduct a comprehensive assessment of your dietary habits, medical history, lifestyle factors, and health goals. You'll discuss your concerns and challenges, and together you'll begin developing a personalized nutrition plan that fits your needs.

To make the most of your consultation, please bring any recent medical records, a list of medications or supplements you're taking, and a 3-day food diary if possible. Come prepared to discuss your health history, current diet, and specific nutrition goals.

We understand that schedules can change. We request at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee. Please contact our office as soon as possible if you need to adjust your appointment time.

We accept a variety of insurance plans for nutrition consultations. Coverage depends on your specific plan and benefits. We recommend contacting your insurance provider to verify coverage before your appointment. We'll provide you with the necessary documentation for reimbursement.
